Welcome to the department of CSE

Mohammadpur Kendriya College wasestablished in 1969 with a mission and vision to develop world-class human resources. With this continuation, Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) startedin the session 1998-99 with a vision to meet national development needs and global challenges in IT through quality teaching, learning, research and knowledge dissemination. As a result, hundreds of graduates have been produced who are occupying top positions in the country and abroad.

After two decades of glorious journey, our department continues to work with reputation under the visionary direction of the esteemed Principal Md. Syed Jafar Ali and the experienced Governing Body.
